Switcheroo

Empower your power users

Switcheroo is a fuzzy search solution that helps your users navigate your web application faster. Your users are everything to your business and let's face it, sometimes putting all of the functionality at a single click just isn't possible, that's where Switcheroo makes your users lives awesome! With a quick key command or button click your users can find what they need fast. No searching through menus, no navigating through multiple pages, just type, select, done.
